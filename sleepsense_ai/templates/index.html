🕐

Consistent Schedule

Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day — even on weekends. This reinforces your circadian rhythm.

📵

Limit Screens Before Bed

Avoid phones and laptops at least 60 minutes before sleep. Blue light suppresses melatonin production.

🌡

Cool & Dark Environment

Keep bedroom between 18–20°C. Darkness signals the brain it's time to sleep. Use blackout curtains if needed.

☕

Caffeine Curfew

Stop consuming caffeine after 2 PM. Coffee's half-life is 5–6 hours — it can still be in your system at midnight.

🧘

Relaxation Techniques

Practice 4-7-8 breathing, progressive muscle relaxation, or guided meditation to wind down before bed.

🏃

Daily Exercise

Regular physical activity improves sleep quality. Aim for 30+ min/day but avoid intense workouts within 3 hrs of bedtime.

🥗

Watch Your Diet

Avoid heavy meals 2–3 hours before bed. Magnesium-rich foods (nuts, leafy greens) promote deep sleep.

📓

Sleep Diary

Track your sleep patterns daily — duration, quality, mood. Identifying trends helps you make better lifestyle changes.
